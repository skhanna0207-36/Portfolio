Welcome to My Trading Journey

Trading is something I'm still learning about in 2026. As a computer science student, I find the technical aspects interesting, but I'm definitely not an expert yet. With markets being so volatile due to geopolitical tensions between US, Iran, and Israel, I'm trying to understand how to navigate these uncertain times. This page is where I share what I've learned so far and track my progress. The current market environment has made me realize that understanding both technical analysis and fundamental factors is crucial for success. Oil prices continue to be a major driver of market movements, and as India is a major oil importer, any geopolitical tension can impact our economy significantly. I'm starting with small paper trades to get the hang of it, focusing on understanding rather than making quick profits. The journey has been challenging but rewarding, as I'm learning to analyze charts, read financial statements, and most importantly, manage my emotions during market volatility. Every trading day brings new lessons, and I'm documenting them here to help others who might be on a similar learning path.

Current Market Events

In 2026, I'm seeing how crude oil prices and geopolitical tensions continue to affect markets. The ongoing situations between US, Iran, and Israel keep oil prices volatile, which directly impacts India's economy. Oil remains a major import, so price spikes cause inflation and weaken the rupee. It's challenging for someone new to trading to navigate these conditions!

What we're witnessing in 2026 shows how interconnected global events are with financial markets. I'm trying to understand how these events impact different sectors - airlines suffer from high oil prices, oil companies benefit, and consumer goods face pressure from inflation. It's a complex balancing act that I'm still trying to understand.
