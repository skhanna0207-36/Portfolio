Personal Finance Topics

In 2026, finance has become even more important to understand with the current geopolitical climate. As a student, I want to learn about managing money wisely during uncertain times. With inflation and market volatility, saving and investing for the future feels more challenging than ever. These are some of the topics I've been researching to protect my financial future.

Finance Concepts I'm Learning

  • Budgeting - Tracking expenses carefully in inflationary times
  • Saving strategies - Building emergency funds for uncertain times
  • Investment basics - Diversifying to reduce geopolitical risks
  • Compound interest - How money grows over time (if it survives inflation!)
  • Tax basics - Understanding how taxes affect take-home pay

Financial Goals

  • Emergency Fund - Save 6 months of living expenses
  • Education - Save for post-graduate studies
  • First Investment - Start with a mutual fund SIP
  • Retirement - Learn about long-term investing

Resources I'm Using

  • Books like "Rich Dad Poor Dad" by Robert Kiyosaki
  • Online courses on financial literacy and market analysis
  • Financial news websites for tracking geopolitical events
  • YouTube channels about personal finance in uncertain times

What I've Learned So Far

In 2026, I've learned that starting early is even more important. With inflation eating away at savings, compound interest needs to work even harder to keep up. Every rupee saved today is worth less tomorrow, so I need to be smart about where I invest.

Clear goals are more crucial than ever in uncertain times. Whether it's saving for education or building an emergency fund, knowing what I'm working toward helps me stay focused when markets are volatile.
